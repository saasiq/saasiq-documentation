# 📖 Glossary

## Overview

A comprehensive glossary of terms, acronyms, and concepts used throughout SaaSIQ. Organized alphabetically for quick lookup.

---

## A

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Active User** | A user who has logged into a SaaS application at least once in the past 30 days. |
| **AI Copilot** | SaaSIQ's conversational AI assistant that answers natural language questions about your SaaS portfolio. |
| **AI Insights** | Machine-learning-generated recommendations for cost savings, renewals, and negotiations. |
| **Alert** | A system notification triggered by a threshold breach, compliance event, or AI-detected anomaly. |
| **App Utilization** | Percentage of licensed seats that are actively used, measured over 30/60/90-day windows. |
| **Approval Flow** | The process of moving a Shadow IT app to "Managed" status after admin review. |

---

## B

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Benchmark** | Industry-average pricing or utilization data used to compare your costs against market norms. |
| **Billing Cycle** | The recurring interval (monthly/annual) at which a SaaS vendor charges for their service. |
| **Block** | An action that marks a Shadow IT app as prohibited and flags it for removal. |
| **Business Plan** | SaaSIQ's mid-tier subscription plan covering up to 500 SaaS applications. |

---

## C

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Compliance** | Adherence to regulatory frameworks (SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA, ISO 27001) and internal policies. |
| **Confidence Score** | A percentage (0–100%) indicating how certain an AI recommendation is. Scores above 85% are high-confidence. |
| **Contract** | A formal agreement with a SaaS vendor specifying terms, pricing, duration, and renewal conditions. |
| **Cost Optimization** | The process of reducing SaaS spend through license reclamation, downgrades, renegotiation, or consolidation. |
| **Critical Alert** | The highest-severity alert, indicating an issue requiring immediate attention (e.g., data breach, expired compliance cert). |

---

## D

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Dashboard** | The main overview screen showing KPIs, charts, urgent actions, and quick navigation. |
| **Data Residency** | The geographic location where a SaaS vendor stores and processes customer data. |
| **Department Cost** | Total SaaS spend attributed to a specific business department. |
| **Discovery** | The automated process of detecting all SaaS applications in use across the organization. |
| **DPA** | **Data Processing Agreement** — a legal contract between a data controller and data processor, required under GDPR. |

---

## E–F

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Encryption** | The process of encoding data so only authorized parties can read it. SaaSIQ uses AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit. |
| **Enterprise Plan** | SaaSIQ's top-tier subscription plan with unlimited apps, dedicated support, and custom integrations. |
| **Framework** | A compliance standard (SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA, ISO 27001) against which applications are evaluated. |

---

## G–H

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **GDPR** | **General Data Protection Regulation** — EU data privacy law requiring consent, data minimization, and breach notification. |
| **HIPAA** | **Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act** — US healthcare data protection standard. |
| **HR Sync** | Integration with your HRIS that automatically triggers offboarding when an employee exits. |

---

## I–J

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Integration** | A connection between SaaSIQ and an external service (e.g., Google Workspace, Okta, Jira) to import data. |
| **ISO 27001** | International information security management standard. |

---

## K–L

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **KPI** | **Key Performance Indicator** — a measurable value displayed on dashboards (e.g., Total Spend, Active Apps). |
| **License** | A seat or subscription unit purchased from a SaaS vendor. Can be active, unused, or over-provisioned. |
| **License Reclamation** | The process of recovering unused licenses to reduce costs. |

---

## M–N

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Managed App** | A SaaS application officially approved and tracked by IT administration. |
| **MFA** | **Multi-Factor Authentication** — a security mechanism requiring two or more verification methods. |
| **Negotiation Brief** | An AI-generated document with market benchmarks, usage data, and talking points for vendor negotiations. |

---

## O–P

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Offboarding** | Revoking SaaS access for departing employees and transferring or archiving their data. |
| **Organization** | The top-level entity in SaaSIQ representing a company or business unit. |
| **Policy** | A governance rule that automatically evaluates SaaS applications against defined criteria (compliance, spending, data residency). |
| **Power User** | An employee with the highest engagement score across SaaS applications. |

---

## Q–R

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **RBAC** | **Role-Based Access Control** — assigning permissions based on user roles (Admin, Manager, Analyst, Viewer). |
| **Renewal** | The event when a SaaS contract reaches its end date and must be renewed, renegotiated, or cancelled. |
| **Risk Score** | A composite score (0–100) measuring an organization's overall compliance and security posture. |

---

## S

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **SaaS** | **Software as a Service** — cloud-based software delivered over the internet on a subscription basis. |
| **Shadow IT** | SaaS applications adopted by employees without IT department approval or oversight. |
| **SOC 2** | **Service Organization Control 2** — an auditing framework for trust services criteria (security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, privacy). |
| **Spend Intelligence** | SaaSIQ's analytics module for analyzing, forecasting, and optimizing SaaS spend. |
| **SSO** | **Single Sign-On** — authentication that allows one login to access multiple applications. |

---

## T–U

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **TLS** | **Transport Layer Security** — cryptographic protocol securing data in transit. SaaSIQ requires TLS 1.3. |
| **Utilization Rate** | (Active Users ÷ Licensed Seats) × 100%. Below 50% is considered underutilized. |

---

## V–Z

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Vendor** | A company that provides SaaS products (e.g., Slack, Salesforce, Figma). |
| **Waste** | SaaS spending on unused or underutilized licenses and redundant subscriptions. |
| **Webhook** | An HTTP callback that sends real-time data to external services when events occur in SaaSIQ. |
