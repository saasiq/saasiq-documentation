# 🔧 Administration Module

**Platform settings, alerts, team management, and org-level controls**

`Home` · **Administration**

</div>

---

## Overview

The Administration module manages the **platform itself** — alerts, settings, team management, and organization-level controls. It's where admins configure SaaSIQ to fit their organization's needs.

---

## What's Inside

| Feature | Purpose | Key Question It Answers |
|---------|---------|------------------------|
| [Alerts & Notifications](alerts-notifications.md) | Real-time alert feed and notification preferences | *"What needs my attention right now?"* |
| [Settings](settings.md) | All platform configuration — org, integrations, team, security, billing | *"How do I configure SaaSIQ?"* |
| [Organization Management](organization-management.md) | Multi-org support, user profile, help center | *"How do I manage multiple organizations?"* |

---

## Who Uses This Module?

| Role | What They Access |
|------|-----------------|
| **Admin** | All settings, all alerts, user management, billing |
| **Manager** | Alerts, view settings, limited team management |
| **Viewer** | Own alerts only, view-only access to settings |
